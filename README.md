## Overview

This project involves an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze and visualize employee promotion trends across various dimensions, including departments, regions, and recruitment channels. The primary goal is to enable data-driven HR decision-making and ensure fair, performance-based promotion processes.

## Key Insights & Features

The dashboard analyzes multiple factors to uncover patterns in employee advancement:

* **Performance as a Predictor:** Reveals a strong correlation between employees' previous-year performance ratings and their promotion outcomes.
* **Education Impact:** Demonstrates that employees with higher education levels (e.g., Master's degree) have a greater probability of promotion.
* **Recruitment Channel Effectiveness:** Shows that referred candidates often have better retention rates and stronger promotion outcomes compared to other channels.
* **Regional Skill Gaps:** Identifies specific regions with potential skill gaps or higher training needs, allowing for targeted development programs.

## Business Outcome

This analytics tool empowers HR leaders and managers to:

✅ **Identify High-Potential Talent:** Proactively spot high-performing employees who are ready for advancement.
✅ **Design Equitable Policies:** Use data to build fair and transparent promotion criteria, reducing bias.
✅ **Track Performance & ROI:** Monitor overall workforce performance and measure the effectiveness (ROI) of training and development programs.
